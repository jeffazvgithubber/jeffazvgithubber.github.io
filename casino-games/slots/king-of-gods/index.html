King of Gods is a 50 line game striking thunderous wins into Chumba! Zeus is Wild and substitutes for most symbols. The lightning bolt scatter and lightning chest are the exceptions, as they have their own epic rewards in store for you.

Spin up 3 or more lightning bolts and be awarded 10 free spins. During the free spins, Zeus could strike down on the reels and reward you with more epic Wild wins for the remaining free spins.

King of Gods also offers a unique new bonus feature at Chumba Casino. If you land 3 treasure chests on reels 2,3 and 4 you trigger the Lightning Chest Bonus. Bonus prizes are revealed when you pick a chest and you get 3 picks!
