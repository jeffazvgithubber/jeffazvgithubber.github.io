Dragon’s River is one of our most exciting slots games yet. A first for Chumba – a 720 Ways game. All wins pay left to right AND right to left, except for the Scatter.

According to ancient Chinese mythology, the Dragon’s Gate is located at the top of a waterfall, cascading down a legendary mountain. Many carp attempt the final leap over the waterfall, but few succeed. If a carp successfully makes the jump, it is transformed into a powerful dragon.

The Dragon is Wild and appears on reels 2, 3 & 4. Landing 3 Scatters on the same reels triggers the feature awarding 10 free spins. During your free spins, the carp fish symbols that land on those reels become Wild Dragons! This could land you the mother of all dragons!
